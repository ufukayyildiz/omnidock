# Code Of Conduct

OmniDock is an open-source project for people building self-hosted email operations on Cloudflare. Keep discussion practical, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and clear.
- Assume good intent.
- Share enough technical detail to help reproduce issues.
- Avoid posting secrets, private emails, private domains, or personal data.
- Keep feedback about code, documentation, security, design, and product behavior specific.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Posting another person's private data.
- Publishing exploit details or credentials in public issues.
- Spam, unrelated promotion, or repeated low-effort comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or escalate security-sensitive reports to private channels when needed.
